Technical Support FAQ
=====================

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. The reset link expires in 24 hours.

Q: What browsers are supported?
A: We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach us via:
   - Email: support@acme-tech.example.com
   - Phone: 1-800-ACME-HELP (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST)
   - Live chat: Available on our website during business hours

Q: What is the API rate limit?
A: Rate limits depend on your plan:
   - Starter: 100 requests/minute
   - Professional: 1000 requests/minute
   - Enterprise: Custom limits

Q: How do I export my data?
A: Go to Settings > Data Management > Export. You can export in CSV, JSON, or XML format. Large exports are sent via email.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use AES-256 encryption, regular security audits, and are SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is backed up daily.
